My Thesis

🏫 BS-IT Thesis · ACLC College of Balanga · 2026
OraniLink
A Community Information & Connectivity Platform for Orani, Bataan

OraniLink is a web-based community platform designed to connect the residents, local businesses, and government services of Orani, Bataan in one centralized, accessible digital space.

The project addresses the lack of a local information hub — residents often struggle to find official announcements, local services, or connect with their barangay offices. OraniLink bridges that gap with a platform built to be lightweight and accessible even on low-end devices.

Community announcements & news feed
Local business & services directory
Barangay information portal
Resident registration & profiles
Mobile-friendly & lightweight design
Admin dashboard for content management
Built with
PHP MySQL HTML CSS JavaScript XAMPP
